Kitchen

This single-storey house has a modern and open plan concept, inspired by the Japandi style. The interior features spacious open spaces that perfectly integrate the living room, dining room, and kitchen. A harmonious blend of Japanese minimalism and Scandinavian simplicity characterizes its design ethos. Clean lines and a neutral color palette create a calm and uncluttered atmosphere throughout the house. Three bedrooms line the sides of the open space, providing a private retreat while maintaining a sense of connection with the central room. Large windows allow abundant natural light to illuminate the interior, enhancing the overall sense of calm and balance within this modern and culturally-infused living space.
