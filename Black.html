Green in Small

A small house built with a floor area of 4x8m. The interior of this house ingeniously merges the family room and kitchen, omitting a formal dining space to maximize the limited square footage. The design emphasizes a practical and efficient layout, aligning with the minimalist ethos of tiny living. A refreshing and vibrant green color palette dominates the interior, creating a sense of openness and connection to nature. The kitchen seamlessly integrates with the family room, fostering a communal atmosphere where residents can engage in various activities without feeling confined by walls. To optimize space utilization, the bathroom is cleverly tucked away beneath the staircase, capitalizing on the often-underutilized area. This design choice not only adds an element of surprise but also expands the usable living space. The green color scheme continues into the bathroom, fostering a cohesive aesthetic throughout the tiny house. Overall, the interior design showcases a thoughtful blend of functionality, style, and eco-friendly elements, making the most of every inch within the tiny home.
